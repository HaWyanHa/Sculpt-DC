DC's Only Cycle and Yoga Studio

A revolution in DC’s boutique fitness scene. Now offering unique fusion formats and strength training. Find your balance between high-intensity and mindful movement. Personalized service with a focus on community building.

Cycle

The dedicated indoor cycle studio houses 35 of the latest Schwinn cycles equipped with RPM monitoring computers. Bikes are fitted with SPD clips or conventional cages that are appropriate for stiff soled sneakers. LED lighting, the latest studio design and a high end audio-visual system complement your riding experience.

Yoga

This 600 sq ft room does double duty as a yoga and group fitness room. Holding up to 30 participants, this room can be adjusted to become an intimate candle filled room for yoga & meditation or can be a functional training playground with weights and more to achieve the highest level of circuit style training. Sculpt 360, Bootcamp and Barre classes are held in this room. Yoga mats are $2 to rent of free for our monthly membership holders.

Reception & Bathrooms

The reception area welcomes guests with our friendly staff who are ready to check guests in and answer any questions regarding classes and instructors, Complimentary daily locker service is provided. The staff prepares the fitness rooms prior to guest arrival for classes with towels, mats, and workout equipment.

Spa quality amenities are found in the bathrooms. 2 Showers, 4 bathrooms and a changing area complete the space. Towel rental is available.Use the form below to fill out any questions you may have. A Sculpt DC Advisor will be in touch within 48 hours.

Classes

  • Sculpt 45 Cycle

    Our signature total-body Sculpt Cycle class uses weights and core exercises to improve strength, stability and coordination while increasing endurance in 45 minutes.

  • Sculpt Cycle + Core

    Torch calories and sculpt chiseled abs and a strong back in this class, which couples 40 minutes of intense cycling with 20 minutes of non-stop core training. Using scientifically-proven, highly effective principles of high intensity interval training, along with exercises designed to work your core from every angle, Sculpt Cycle + Core will bring you maximum results in minimal time.

  • Sculpt Fusion

    The perfect union of total body cycling, body weight training, core work, and yoga in a balanced, athletic workout. Each Sculpt Fusion class starts off with a high-intensity cycling session designed to push you to your limits, and then transitions to our yoga studio to top it off with strength moves designed to sculpt toned arms and a chiseled core, and yoga-based stretches designed to release tight hips, hamstrings, and lower back muscles. This highly effective workout yields fast results, and will leave you drenched in sweat and wanting more!

  • Yoga Basics

    Our Sculpt Basics class is designed especially for beginners with alignment cues, demonstrations, and assists to help you develop and deepen your practice. The vinyasa flow and sun salutations are much slower and are broken down posture by posture. This class is also a great refresher for more experienced practitioners to go back to the basics and work on alignment and breathing.

  • Yoga All Levels

    All Levels Yoga takes the traditional vinyasa series and incorporates a more dynamic and athletic flow in an accessible format. This open-level class is the place to be if you want to lose weight, increase strength and flexibility or cross-train. Whether you are new to yoga or a seasoned athlete, come and experience the body transforming, calorie burning and mind calming effects of a Sculpt Yoga practice.

  • Sculpt 360

    Sculpt 360 is a fast-paced, full body workout that combines the best of cardio, strength training and yoga. This class features the use of individual hand-weights (up to 15 lbs) to target all major muscle groups. Exercises such as push-ups, squats, lunges, jumping jacks, and bicep curls will build your overall strength, boost your cardiovascular system and increase lean muscle mass. Add this class to your routine and see the benefits immediately. ALL Levels are welcome, however be prepared to push your limits and SWEAT! SHOES ARE NOT REQUIRED FOR THIS CLASS.

  • Yogalates

    A combination of Pilates & Yoga to stretch and strengthen your body. The emphasis is on core control in all poses and focus on breathing while in motion. ALL levels are welcome in this total body workout.

    • Membership to Sculpt DC Lifestyle Program that entitles you to discounts and savings at area businesses with your Sculpt DC key card. Please see participating businesses below.
    • No need to keep track of remaining passes or account balances.

    Our cancellation window for cycle is 3 hours prior to class start time and yoga is 1 hour prior to class start time.You can cancel a class online, through our FREE mobile app or by calling the studio.

All late cancellations fees will be donated to our charity of the month.

    The membership fees will recur monthly until cancelled by the member in accordance with our cancellation policy (see above). The monthly fees will be charged automatically and approximately on the same day of the month that the original purchase was made.

    This is a special rate with a minimum initial commitment of 3 months and is valid for up to 12 months. This membership is non-transferable and non-refundable. Memberships may be cancelled with 30 days written notice after the initial 3 months. You are still responsible for any payments that fall within the 30 day cancellation period.

Booking Window

Our booking window opens 7 days in advance. So, for example, if you look at our schedule on a Monday, you will only be able to register for classes through the following Monday. Class reservations can be made online, through our FREE mobile app or by calling our studio.

Sharing Memberships

Sharing memberships with your BFF or significant other are totally allowed for 5, 10 and 20 class packages, so share the love! Just keep in mind that a membership cannot be shared if you are on any of our monthly membership options.

Waitlist

Bummer-class is full and now you are on the waitlist. We promise-the waitlist works! As spots open up, we can offer you a spot in the class via email. You must, however, purchase a class BEFORE you can reserve a spot on the wait-list. Emails will be sent up to 2 hours before the start of a class.

×

Late Policy

If you haven’t checked in at the front desk 5 minutes before the start of class, your spot will be released to a fellow client who is on the wait list and wanting to sweat! If you show up after class has started and there are still spots left we will allow you to attend until 5 minutes after the start of a class. Any later and we cannot allow you in due to safety concerns, but are happy to rebook you in another class!

Cancellation Window

Starting May 5th, 2014, our new cancellation window for cycle is 3 hours prior to class start time and yoga is 1 hour prior to class start time. You can cancel a class online, through our FREE mobile app or by calling the studio.

All late cancelations fees will be donated to our charity of the month.

All members on a class package that cancel inside the window will lose a class from their class package.

This fee does not apply to our introductory New Member Offer.

Energy Exchange Program

The Energy Exchange Program at Sculpt DC is unique and very special both to the studio and to the members of the Energy Exchange itself.

It works like this… you work a 3 hour shift on the same day at the same time once a week helping to keep the studio tidy, give tours and/or marketing and in exchange you receive unlimited classes for the duration of your time with us. It’s important to know going in, this is NOT A VOLUNTEER position. We like to think of you as our AMBASSADORS.

It is hard work, the EE positions require commitment, work ethic and positive energy. It’s also a great way to get to know your teachers and be a part of the Sculpt DC community! Please complete the application below.
